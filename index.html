About Us

We are passionate about good food, especially good Spanish food! Spanish tortilla (potato omelette) has always been one of our favourites and we have spent years perfecting our tortilla using the traditional recipe but also by experimenting to create our own specialty tortillas.

We now sell our tortillas from our own tortilla truck at different food markets in London. Come visit us!

We only use the best and freshest ingredients and source them locally when possible. We buy our potatoes, onions, and peppers from local farmers and import our high-quality olive oil, pine nuts, and garlic from Spain.

How to Enjoy Our Tortillas?

Tortillas are amazing on (nearly) any occasion, and therefore the ways to use them are (nearly) unlimited, but below are some ideas if you are not sure what to do with your tortilla!


  • Organise a picnic!
  • Take it with you when you go hiking
  • Enjoy with family and friends
  • Keep it all to yourself!
  • Have it for dinner
  • Make a tortilla bocadillo (sandwich)
